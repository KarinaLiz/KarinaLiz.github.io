The Basic and Present

The Basic

It opened on October 1, 1971 (That's why its 50th anniversary is being celebrated). It is the first Walt Disney World park. It has 6 lands (divisions in the park). It measures 142 acres (0.57 km) (0.22 miles). Currently contains 76 things to do (atractions, entertainment, shows, stores, dining, etc). It has a train that runs around the entire park (it is currently closed and it is not known when it will reopen).

Present

As it is important and interesting to look back and foward in time, it is also important to know what is going on in the present time. Especially now since many entertainment and changes are going on.

50th anniversary celebration in the park

Fab 50

Fab 50 is a collection of golden statues of Disney characters located around the 4 Walt Disney World theme parks. Some of these are easy to spot but some others are a little more trickier to find. In Animal Kingdom, there is a total of 5 statues.
