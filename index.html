The Basic and Present

The Basic

It opened on October 1, 1971 (That's why its 50th anniversary is being celebrated) becoming the first Walt Disney World park. It has 6 lands (divisions in the park): Main Street U.S.A, Adventureland, Frontierland,Liberty Square, Fantasyland and Tommorrowland and as a whole the park measures 142 acres (0.57 km) (0.22 miles). Currently contains 76 things to do (bewtween atractions, entertainment, shows, stores, dining, etc). A train station can be found in the entrance and is home to a classic steam train that can travels around the entire park (it is currently closed and it is not known when it will reopen).

50th anniversary celebration in the park

Fab 50

Fab 50 is a collection of golden statues of Disney characters located around the 4 Walt Disney World theme parks. Some of these are easy to spot but some others are a little more trickier to find. Also, Magic Kingdom has the most statues since the celebrations purpose is that park. In Magic Kingdom, there is a total of 10 statues.

Beacon of Magic

After nightfall, Cinderella's castle is iluminated with Eardescent colors (it is available most nights).

Calvacade

A calvacade is a short version of a parade since there are only a few floats and it ends faster. Disney has used them for some time now.

"Mickey's Celebration Cavalcade"

This calvacade consist of 2 people holding the start of the calvalcade rope, 2 floats (first one with a simple design and the second one in the shape of a gift box), dancers, 8 characters with 50th anniversary clothes 2 people holding the end of the parade rope.

“Disney Adventure Friends Cavalcade!”

This calvacade consist of 2 people holding the start of the calvalcade rope, 2 floats (first one with a simple design and the second one in the shape of a box), dancers, 16 characters and 2 people holding the end of the parade rope.
