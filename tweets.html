The Basic and Present

Basic

It opened on October 1, 1982. It is the second Walt Disney World park. It has 4 lands (divisions in the park), however, with the parks reimagining, most of these will change names. It measures acres ( km) ( miles). Currently contains things to do (atractions, entertainment, shows, stores, dining, etc). It has a monorail that runs around the entire park and to other locations such as thru a hotel.

50th anniversary celebration in the park

Fab 50

Fab 50 is a collection of golden statues of Disney characters located around the 4 Walt Disney World theme parks. Some of these are easy to spot but some others are a little more trickier to find. Also, Magic Kingdom has the most statues since the celebrations purpose is that park. In Magic Kingdom, there is a total of 10 statues.

Beacon of Magic

After nightfall, Spaceship Earth (name of the attraction inside the sphere) is iluminated with Eardescent colors (it is available most nights).

A calvacade is a short version of a parade since there are only a few floats and it ends faster. Disney has used them for some time now.
